  • Who will you meet?

    You will meet with our practice manager and treatment coordinator, who will explain the treatment process. You can ask them any question you have about orthodontic treatment.

  • What will happen?

    Your orthodontic consultation is a chance for us to scan your teeth and jaws to determine what type of orthodontic treatment you could most benefit from.

  • When can you visit?

    Fill in the booking form and let us know when you’d like to come in for your first orthodontic consultation. We’ll do our best to fit in with your schedule.

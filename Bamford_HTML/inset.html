Invisalign For You

If you want straight teeth and a beautiful smile but you’re not keen on wearing traditional metal braces, then Invisalign was designed for you. Bamford Orthodontic Practice in Rochdale specialises in Invisalign - an innovative orthodontic treatment that straightens your teeth with removable plastic aligners instead of traditional metal braces.

What is Invisalign

Invisalign is a virtually invisible orthodontic treatment system that uses thin, removable, clear plastic aligners to gradually shift your teeth. It can help treat a variety of orthodontic concerns including crowding, spacing and crooked teeth. However, because the aligners are removable and comfortable, Invisalign treatment will have very little impact on your daily life.

How does Treatment Work with Invisalign?

If you choose to have Invisalign, we will map out a treatment plan according to our orthodontic assessment and your goals. We will also complete a full digital scan of your teeth using our orthodontic technologies to prepare a 3D representation of your smile before, during and after treatment. This allows you to know what changes to expect during the course of your treatment.

We will then send all the details of your personalised plan to Align Technology, where Invisalign prints your clear aligners so that you can begin treatment. Since these are customised for your teeth alone, as you change your aligners to the next set in the series, your teeth gradually move into the desired positions. You will wear each set for approximately 2 weeks before moving on to the next in the series.

Invisalign FAQ

Will my teeth be straight after treatment?

Yes - in many cases, it’s even better. Invisalign® can solve most of the same issues as traditional fixed braces. The main advantage of Invisalign® is that it is often much more comfortable, convenient and discreet.

The custom-made aligners are created to fit perfectly, using leading technology that measures the contours of your mouth. Plus, you can take them out while you eat and brush your teeth, so you don’t have the same restrictions as with traditional braces.

How do I look after my aligners?

The best way to clean your aligners is by brushing and rinsing them in lukewarm water and using special cleaning tablets. After eating it is important that you brush your teeth before you re-insert your aligners. This has two benefits; it keeps your teeth clean and stops food discolouring the aligners.
